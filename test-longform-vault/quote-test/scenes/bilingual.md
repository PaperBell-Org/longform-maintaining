# Quote & Apostrophe Rendering Test

## 1. English only

He said "hello" and left. A 'single' quoted word follows. It's a "test", don't you think? See O'Brien's rule and a '90s look. The term "important" still matters here.

"Start with a quote," she said, "and end with one."

## 2. 中文 only

他说“你好”然后离开了。这是‘重点’，也是“关键”。她问道：“真的吗？”然后停顿了一下。句首引号：“开始”是这样的；句末引号，他最后说“结束”。

## 3. 中英混排 Mixed

He cited "论文" today and did "important" work. 她说“hello”之后，又强调了“重点”两个字。在中文句子里引用英文 "machine learning" 一词很常见；and in English we sometimes quote a Chinese term like "机器学习" as well.

数据表明，“the effect is real”，而作者称之为“显著”。

## 4. Nested / 嵌套

"He said 'yes' loudly," she reported. “他说‘好的’之后就离开了，”她这样转述。

## 5. Apostrophes / 撇号

Contractions and possessives: it's, don't, can't, we're, O'Brien's data, the students' papers, and the '80s. 中文里不该出现撇号问题：机器学习、深度学习。
